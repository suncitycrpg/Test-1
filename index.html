SUN CITY is a post-apocalyptic RPG game with quests, world map travel, RPG system, leveling, and combat mechanics. SUN CITY game was inspired by Fallout and Wasteland games.

The world of the game is the Great Desert, inhabited by surviving humans (and not only humans). One day, people woke up and saw half-destroyed and abandoned cities, and a vast desert around them. No one remembers what happened yesterday. They had to start all over again. Sixty years have passed since the "Creation of the World"...

You are a villager of the Ariano tribe. Your village has been attacked by raiders, and the village elder asks you to go to Sun City to bring help. The fate of the village... and the world... depends on you.

For now, while we all wait for the release, you can complete tasks, earn points, and make donations. Later on, when the game is released, accumulated points can be exchanged for valuable in-game items and cryptocurrency.
